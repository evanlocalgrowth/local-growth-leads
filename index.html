About

Who We Are

Local Growth Leads was founded with a simple mission: help local service businesses grow by connecting them with real customers who are actively searching for the services they provide. We build and rank high-quality digital assets that capture genuine demand and deliver exclusive, ready-to-book leads directly to our partners.

The company was created by a Morehouse College engineering student who is passionate about building systems that support small businesses, strengthen communities, and create long-term digital value.

How it works

We handle the marketing. You answer the phone.

Simple, transparent, and built around how local businesses actually operate.

1

We build and rank local service sites.

We create clean, trustworthy websites designed around the exact services you offer. Then we optimize them so they show up when people in your area search for things like “roof repair near me” or “junk removal today.” The goal is simple: put your business in front of people who are already ready to hire someone.

2

Homeowners submit real service requests.

When someone needs help, they fill out a quick quote form or call directly from the site. These aren’t casual browsers — they’re people actively looking for the exact service you provide.

3

We send those leads straight to you.

Every call or form submission goes directly to your business. You’re the only one receiving them. No shared lists, no bidding against other companies, and no middlemen getting in the way.
